Password Strength Criteria:

Length: Password should be at least 8 characters long (though longer is better).
Uppercase Letters: Should contain at least one uppercase letter.
Lowercase Letters: Should contain at least one lowercase letter.
Numbers: Should contain at least one numeric digit.
Special Characters: Should contain at least one special character (e.g., !@#$%^&*).








Password Strength Evaluation:

Weak: Missing several criteria (e.g., too short, lacks complexity).
Medium: Meets some criteria but could be stronger.
Strong: Meets all criteria and is of good length.
